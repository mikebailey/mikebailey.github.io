The [Social Capital Atlas](https://socialcapital.org) is a public dataset and interactive tool, built with Raj Chetty and Opportunity Insights, measuring three forms of social capital &mdash; economic connectedness, social cohesion, and civic engagement &mdash; across nearly every U.S. ZIP code, high school, and college. The underlying research showed that economic connectedness is one of the strongest community-level predictors of upward mobility in the United States.

**Companion papers** (both *Nature* 608(7921), 2022):

- *Social Capital I: Measurement and Associations with Economic Mobility.* [[Nature]](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-022-04996-4)
- *Social Capital II: Determinants of Economic Connectedness.* [[Nature]](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-022-04997-3)
